About Us

Founded in San Francisco, CA, Spicy Indian Bite provides authentic Indian meals that are cooked daily (based on the orders received). We do not cook in advance therefore offering fresh meals at a very reasonable price compared to your local restaurants. With over 20 years of cooking experience in the restaurant industry, our Head Chef Mr. Pargat Sangha brings years of expertise and flavors that are most certainly hard to find in the Bay Area.
